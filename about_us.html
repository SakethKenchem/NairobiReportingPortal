About The System

This system is a platform for the residents of Nairobi to report any issues they may have to the county government. The system is also a platform for the county government to communicate with the residents of Nairobi.

The system is a web application that is accessible to all residents of Nairobi. The system is accessible through the internet and can be accessed through any device that has a web browser.

About The Developers

This system was built by Saketh Kenchem and Raymond Mungai Benaiah, students at Strathmore University. It was built to fulfill the partial requirements of the Diploma in Business Information Technology in the academic year 2022/2023.
